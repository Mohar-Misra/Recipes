This stuffed eggplant recipe combines hearty Italian sausage, fresh vegetables, and aromatic basil with tender eggplant shells. Topped with a crunchy blend of panko bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese, it’s a satisfying and flavorful dish that’s perfect for a main course or as a delightful appetizer.





These grilled stuffed poblano peppers are filled with a flavorful mix of black beans, corn, and fresh herbs, then topped with a creamy pepper Jack cheese sauce. Perfect for a cookout, this dish can easily accommodate vegan and omnivore guests alike. Serve with optional garnishes like pico de gallo and lime wedges.





These air-fried Italian stuffed tomatoes feature a savory mix of brown rice, Parmesan, goat cheese, and toasted walnuts, flavored with garlic and fresh basil. The tomatoes are crisped to perfection in the air fryer, with a golden breadcrumb topping. Quick and flavorful, these make an excellent side dish or light main course.
